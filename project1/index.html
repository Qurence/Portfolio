Fairy Forest

This ancient forest, shrouded in eternal gloom, is hidden from prying eyes somewhere on the edge of the real world and the magical one. Its paths meander like a living thing, changing direction for each traveler, and the air is filled with the sweet scent of flowers that have never seen sunlight. The Forest of Shimmering Shadows is lit only by the soft light of fireflies that swirl around old trees whose branches reach upward as if trying to touch the moon.

Sova

An ancient guardian of the forest with a body resembling a tangle of roots and branches, glowing eyes reflecting the wisdom of the ages, and lights swirling around him as symbols of his magical power.

Olen

The mysterious spirit of the night forest, with fiery eyes and a body woven of shadows and branches, guards the harmony of nature.
